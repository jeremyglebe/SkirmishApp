The Enhanced Unit Cost Rule is an optional rule that adds an additional cost to a unit's total cost based on the number of special rules it has.

The additional cost is calculated as ((number of special rules) - 3) * 10. This rule only applies to special rules with non-negative cost and only if the number of special rules with non-negative cost exceeds 3.

This rule is intended to provide a more balanced cost structure for units with many special rules, and to encourage players to choose a smaller number of high-impact special rules rather than many low-impact ones.
